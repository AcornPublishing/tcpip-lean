Analogue

The ChipWeb™ server uses the Microchip PIC16F877 on-chip analogue-to-digital converter (ADC) to read the settings of two potentiometers on the demonstration board. The voltage of the two channels (0-5V) is indicated in graphical form on the right.

Adjust one of the two potentiometers, and after a few seconds the graphical indicators will be updated to the new value.
